Xyber vs Oblivion Network Wargame

An environment where MITM attacks can occur. Xyber and Oblivion was one of my first multi-network projects I've built. Both corporates (xyber.org and oblivion.org) manage their own AD, DNS, Apache server, and firewall performing NAT — all routed through a core network. This allows both organizations to communicate with one another, simulating realistic enterprise environments.

A Kali Linux box sits strategically between them, leveraging its position to perform man-in-the-middle attacks and intercept traffic flowing across networks.
